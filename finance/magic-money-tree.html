üî¨ The Magic Formula

Here's how the magic works:

A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt) + PMT √ó (((1 + r/n)^(nt) - 1) / (r/n))

  • A = Final Amount (your tree!)
  • P = Initial Deposit (your first seed)
  • r = Yearly Interest Rate (sunlight!)
  • n = Times interest is paid per year (usually 12 for monthly)
  • t = Years (how long to grow)
  • PMT = Monthly additions (watering!)
