ğŸ“ The 50/30/20 Rule

A simple way to budget:

  • 50% Needs: Housing, food, utilities, insurance - things you MUST have
  • 30% Wants: Entertainment, dining out, hobbies - things you enjoy
  • 20% Savings: Emergency fund, retirement, paying off debt
ğŸ¯ This is a guideline, not a rule! Adjust based on your situation and goals.

  1. Know your income: Take-home pay, not gross

  6. Review: Check monthly, adjust as needed
