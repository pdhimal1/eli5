üñ•Ô∏è How It Actually Works

A Quad Tree is a tree data structure where each node has exactly 4 children. It's used to partition a two-dimensional space by recursively subdividing it into four quadrants.


Why is it useful? Finding something in a regular list can be slow (you might have to check EVERY item). With a Quad Tree, you can quickly eliminate HUGE areas that don't contain what you're looking for!
