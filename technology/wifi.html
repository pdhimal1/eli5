üì° What is WiFi Actually?

WiFi uses radio waves - the same kind that radios and phones use!

But WiFi uses special frequencies (2.4 GHz or 5 GHz) that can carry more information faster.

2.4 GHz = Goes further, slower, more crowded (like a crowded highway)

5 GHz = Doesn't go as far, but much faster (like an empty race track)
