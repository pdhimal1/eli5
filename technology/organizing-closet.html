üéØ Method 1: Sort by Color (Like a Rainbow!)

This is like Bubble Sort - you compare things next to each other!

1. Find two shirts next to each other

2. Which one should come first? (Red before Orange before Yellow)

3. Swap if they're in the wrong order!

4. Repeat until done!

üèÉ Like sorting cards by color - you compare each pair and swap if needed!

üì¶ Method 2: First In, First Out (FIFO)

Think about clothes you wear most often!

The idea: Put the clothes you wear most often in front!

This is like a Queue - like people in a line!

First person in line gets served first!

It's also like a Cache in a computer - the stuff you use most goes in "quick access"!

üè∑Ô∏è Method 3: Group Similar Items

This is like Hash Tables - organizing by categories!

All shirts in one pile, pants in another, socks in another!

When you need pants, you go straight to the pants pile!

This is super fast - just like a dictionary!

üîç Method 4: Binary Search (The Fast Way!)

Imagine your clothes are sorted by color in a line!

Instead of checking every single one, you check the middle!

Is the one you're looking for redder or bluer than the middle?

Then you only look at half! Then half of that!

Boom! You find it super fast!
