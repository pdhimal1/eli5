üî§ What is LLM?

LLM = Large Language Model

It's a computer program that has "read" billions of text documents from the internet!

It learned patterns in language - how words connect, grammar, facts, and even jokes!

When you chat with it, it predicts what word comes next - like autocomplete on superpowers!
