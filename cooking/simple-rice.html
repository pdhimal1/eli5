Why Rice is Great

Rice is a staple for over half the world's population!

It goes with almost anything - vegetables, protein, eggs, stir-fry!

üõí What You Need

  • 1 cup long-grain rice (white or brown)
  • 2 cups water (or broth for more flavor)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter (optional)

üë®‚Äçüç≥ The Ratio

The golden rule: 1 cup rice : 2 cups water

Same ratio works for most types!

Steps:

1. Rinse rice in cold water until water runs clear (gets rid of extra starch!)

2. Add rice + water + salt to pot

3. Bring to boil

4. Cover, turn to LOW

5. Cook 18 minutes (white) or 40 minutes (brown)

6. Turn off heat, let sit 5 min, then FLUFF!
