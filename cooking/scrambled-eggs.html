Why Eggs are Amazing

Eggs are the perfect food - they're fast, cheap, and delicious!

Gordon Ramsay: "The key to perfect scrambled eggs is LOW heat and patience."

Classic (Gordon Ramsay style):

1. Whisk eggs in a bowl with salt and pepper

2. Melt butter in pan over LOW heat

3. Add eggs, stir CONSTANTLY with spatula

4. Remove when still slightly wet (carryover cooking!)

5. Add cold butter, stir to cool down

Easy method:

1. Crack eggs in pan, cook, scramble

2. More rustic, quicker
