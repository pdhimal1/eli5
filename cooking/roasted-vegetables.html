Why Roast?

Roasting is like magic for vegetables! High heat makes the natural sugars caramelize - that's why roasted veggies are sweet and yummy!

Adam Ragusa says: "Roasting is the easiest way to make vegetables delicious. Just cut, oil, salt, and roast!"

üõí What You Need

  • Any vegetables you like! (carrots, broccoli, potatoes, cauliflower, brussels sprouts)
  • Olive oil (enough to lightly coat)
  • Salt
  • optional: pepper, garlic powder, italian seasoning
