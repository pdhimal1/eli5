What is Tofu?

Tofu is made from soybeans - it's like cheese made from plants!

It's bland by itself but absorbs flavors beautifully!

üßä Types of Tofu

  • Silken (soft): Smooth, creamy. Good for soups, smoothies
  • Firm: Holds shape better. Good for stir-fries
  • Extra firm: Best for cooking! Holds shape, gets crispy
  • Super firm: Already pressed, ready to cook

üë®‚Äçüç≥ The Most Important Step: PRESS!

Tofu has water in it. If you don't press it, it'll be soggy!

How to press: Wrap tofu in paper towels, put something heavy on top, wait 20-30 minutes.

Or: Buy super-firm tofu that doesn't need pressing!

üí° How to Make Tofu Delicious:
1. Press out the water!
2. Cut into similar-sized pieces
3. Marinate at least 15 minutes (longer = more flavor)
4. Get the pan HOT
5. Don't touch! Let it get crispy

üî™ Cutting Tofu

Cubes: Great for stir-fries, salads

Slices: Good for sandwiches, grilling

Crumbled: Great for pasta sauce, tacos

Strips: Good for baking, marinating

ü•ò Simple Marinades

Asian: Soy sauce + sesame oil + ginger + garlic

BBQ: BBQ sauce + smoked paprika

Italian: Olive oil + Italian seasoning + garlic

Spicy: Sriracha + soy sauce + lime

Tip: Frozen and thawed tofu has a chewier texture some people prefer! Try it both ways!
