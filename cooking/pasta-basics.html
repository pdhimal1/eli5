Why Pasta is Great

Pasta is like a blank canvas - you can add anything to it!

Gordon Ramsay says: "Pasta is about simplicity. Good pasta, good olive oil, good cheese. That's it!"

üõí What You Need

  • 1 lb dried pasta (spaghetti, penne, fusilli)
  • Salt (lots of it! pasta water should taste like the sea)
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil (optional)

üë®‚Äçüç≥ The Secret Steps

  1. Big pot: Use lots of water! 4-6 quarts per pound of pasta.
  2. Salt heavily: Like the ocean! This is your only chance to season the pasta.
  3. Don't add oil: You don't need it! Just stir occasionally.
  4. Test early: Start testing 2 minutes before the package says.
  5. SAVE THE WATER! This is the magic liquid. Save 1 cup before draining!
  6. Finish in pan: Toss pasta with sauce, add splash of starchy water!
üí° Why Starchy Water is Magic!
When you cook pasta, starch gets into the water. That starchy water helps sauces cling to pasta and makes them silky smooth!
Mike the Home Cook: "That pasta water is liquid gold!"
