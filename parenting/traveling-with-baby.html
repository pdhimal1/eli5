ğŸ›« Flying with Baby

  • Book smart: Red-eye flights = more sleep. Direct flights = less stress.
  • Timing: Book during baby's typical nap time
  • At security: Baby can stay in carrier through metal detector
  • During takeoff/landing: Nurse, bottle, or give pacifier - helps with ear pressure
  • Seat choice: Window seat = easier to step out. Aisle = easier to stand. Middle = worst of both!
